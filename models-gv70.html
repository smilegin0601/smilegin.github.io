THE GENESIS GV70

Based on the Genesis design philosophy ‘Athletic Elegance’, the GV70, the first ever mid-sized Genesis SUV, explores new ground outside our design territory.

Design

From the unique, athletic exterior to the minimalist yet driver-focused interior, the Genesis GV70 delivers new inspiration to your daily life.

Safety

An advanced driving assistance system for safe and convenient driving aids the driver in responding to any possible risk.

Technology

With a simple and intuitive operating environment, the Genesis GV70 delivers a new level of impression by responding one step ahead of the driver's needs.

Performance

The driving performance features of the GV70 enable consistently stable and accurate driving even under constantly changing driving and road conditions.
